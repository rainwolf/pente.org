My only options were to reboot, standby, wait 3 hours for the screen to turn off again and try again or set that time to one minute if I could make out enough on the screen to do so and try again after one minute.

Maybe you have the same problem as I do, but I couldn't find anything on the web on this.

Well, I 've had it, and with some research on usenet I've made this little thingy that 'll turn off your screen on a click (some modifications can easily be made to do that on startup) and turn it back on again on another click or on moving your mouse. Delphi source code included.
